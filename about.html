Our story

Rooted in curiosity, driven by clarity.

JoshTeck Company began as a weekend experiment and quickly grew into a collection of thoughtful web pages. Every release aims to make the internet easier for the next visitor.

Where we started

We launched the first JoshTeck page to help friends showcase their work online. The positive feedback inspired us to continue building, reusing components, and sharing what we learned.

Where we're going

Today, the focus is on lightweight, maintainable sites that anyone can run. This refactor brings all of our pages together so you can browse them without losing context.

Values we reference daily

  • Clarity first: copy and layouts stay simple.
  • Consistency: shared components keep pages aligned.
  • Care: every visitor should feel at home.
